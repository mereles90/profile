About Me

I was born in Argentina and this year I've relocated to Europe to look for new experiences, meet new people and improve the skills of my profession.

I finished my studies of Film & TV Production in the National University of Córdoba, one of the most prestigious of Argentina and one of the oldest of America.

I love filmmaking and I try to learn as much as possible about it because I believe everything can contribute to the finish product, giving all my best in every project.
