MARCOS MERELES

Born in Argentina, based in London. After he finished his studies in Film and TV production in the National University of Cordoba, he made his short film debut "Nochebuena", that was picked up in Cannes by a french distribution company. In 2016, he shot "Florinda & Floriana" in Italy and "De Dode Spreekt" in the Netherlands, which premiered in the Imagine Film Festival at the EYE Filmmuseum in Amsterdam. Now he's developing a new sci-fi project, "Anywhere".
